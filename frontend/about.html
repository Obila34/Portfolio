About Me

I'm Sam Obila Allela — a Mathematics & Computer Science student from Nairobi, Kenya. I build machine learning projects, craft front-end interfaces, and believe deeply that technology education should reach every student, no matter their circumstances.
My journey hasn't been a straight line. I've faced real financial barriers in my education, which means I know firsthand what it feels like to want access to learning and have doors closed. That experience didn't defeat me — it sharpened my purpose. Today, I channel that drive into two things: crafting machine learning projects and front-end interfaces that demonstrate what's possible, and teaching others — especially young people in communities who've been told technology isn't for them — that it absolutely is. I share my work publicly on LinkedIn and GitHub not to impress, but to inspire. Every project I post is a message to someone watching: "You can build this too."
